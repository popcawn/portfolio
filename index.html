About Me

Hello, I am Jaavid Malette, a Durham native with a deep passion for technology. From an early age, I was hands-on with computers—building, fixing, and interacting with various operating systems and software. During my senior year of high school, I turned this passion into an IT internship at Triangle Ecycling, a local Durham business dedicated to giving back to the community and recycling electronic waste. This experience further developed my technology skills and deepened my commitment to environmental sustainability.


While interning, I balanced work and college studies at Durham Tech, where I simultaneously gained hands-on experience and education.


Throughout my journey, cybersecurity reappeared. A field that first caught my interest during an 11th-grade civics project where my friend and I created a fictional cybersecurity/police hybrid company, Exclusive Security Corp., with the tagline "Your Protection Guaranteed." (Fun fact: there's a hidden YouTube link related to this within my portfolio.) While my friend pursued a career as a police officer, I pursued cybersecurity.


Since then, I have successfully passed my CompTIA Security+ certification and taken multiple cybersecurity courses, continually expanding my knowledge in the field. I enjoy working on projects and challenging myself with new endeavors, constantly pushing myself to learn and grow in cybersecurity.


In this portfolio, I aim to demonstrate my dedication to cybersecurity through various projects and certifications I’ve earned. Thank you for visiting!
